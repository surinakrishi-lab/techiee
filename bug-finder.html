Frequently Asked Questions

How does Bug Finder integrate with our existing CI/CD?

Bug Finder plugs into most CI/CD pipelines — GitHub Actions, GitLab CI, Jenkins and more — with a lightweight config. Most teams run their first scan within the hour.

What kind of issues does it catch?

Logic errors, memory and resource leaks, security vulnerabilities, and regressions across web and mobile codebases — with clear, traceable reports.

Does it replace our QA team?

No — it's built to support your QA and engineering teams, catching issues earlier so your team can focus on higher-value testing and release work.

Is our code ever stored or shared?

Your code stays within your environment and pipeline; scans run in isolated, secure contexts. Full details are covered in a live walkthrough with our team.
